Welcome to **Hack0Guide**! 

This project was born out of a simple idea: bringing the best resources of the homebrew community together into one cohesive, easily accessible space. By combining the preservation spirit of classic resources like `hacks.guide` and `cfw.guide` with our own original content, we are aiming to create a seamless, all-in-one destination for console modification.

### What is Live Right Now?
Our initial launch focuses on the core structure for our most requested systems:
* **Nintendo 3DS**: Full setup and custom firmware paths.
* **Nintendo Switch & Wii U**: Optimized entry-level frameworks.
* **Legacy Portals**: Early configurations for DSi, Wii, and PS Vita systems.

### Our Open Source Promise
We do not take full credit for the monumental work done by the developers before us. This archive stands on the shoulders of giants, and we have fully credited the original trailblazers in our root directory. Our goal is strictly to expand, update, and safeguard these guides for the community.

Stay tuned—we are just getting started!
