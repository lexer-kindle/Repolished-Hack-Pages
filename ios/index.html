iOS Hacks Guide

A complete guide to iOS custom firmware and jailbreaking, from stock to CFW. Jailbreaking unlocks your device so you can install tweaks, themes, sideload apps, and run software Apple normally blocks. The right method depends entirely on your device model and iOS version, so start by selecting your device below.
